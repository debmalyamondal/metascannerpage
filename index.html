Frequently Asked Questions

What types of metadata can Metascanner extract?

Metascanner extracts a comprehensive range of data, including EXIF (camera settings, date/time), IPTC (copyright, keywords), and XMP (edit history, author information), providing a complete picture of an image's origin and lifecycle.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security. All uploads are encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not share, sell, or analyze your images for any purpose other than providing you with your requested report. Your data is your own.

Can Metascanner detect if an image has been edited?

Yes. By analyzing metadata tags related to software (like Adobe Photoshop or GIMP) and comparing data inconsistencies, Metascanner can provide strong indicators of digital alteration. Our AI also scans for visual artifacts associated with manipulation.

What file formats are supported?

We support all major image formats, including JPEG, PNG, TIFF, HEIC, and various camera RAW formats (e.g., .CR2, .NEF, .ARW). Our system is continuously updated to support new and emerging file types.
